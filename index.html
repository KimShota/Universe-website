Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2026

U-Niverse ("we", "our", or "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy describes how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use the U-Niverse mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide and data necessary to run the app:

  • Account information: When you sign in with Google, we receive your email address, name, and profile picture from the provider.
  • Profile and usage data: Data you enter in the app, such as your creator goals, content pillars, schedule, mission completion, and notes, is stored to provide the service.
  • Device and app usage: We may receive standard information such as device type and app version to ensure compatibility and stability.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide and personalize the app (e.g., missions, streak, coins, content tips).
  • Authenticate you and keep your account secure.
  • Store your preferences and content so you can use the app across sessions.
  • Improve the app and fix issues.

3. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services that may process your data:

  • Supabase: Hosting for authentication and database storage. Their privacy policy: supabase.com/privacy.
  • Google: For sign-in (OAuth). Google’s privacy policy: policies.google.com/privacy.

We do not sell your personal information to third parties.

4. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored on secure servers provided by Supabase. We use industry-standard practices (including encryption and access controls) to protect your information. Authentication is handled via secure tokens; we do not store your Google password.

5. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, we remove your profile and app data from our systems. Authentication records may be retained by the identity provider (e.g., Google) according to their policies.

6. Your Rights and Choices

You can:

  • Delete your account: Use the "Delete Account" option in the app’s settings to remove your data from our systems.
  • Stop using the app: You can uninstall the app at any time.
  • Contact us: For questions or requests about your data, use the support or contact option in the app or the support URL listed on the app’s store page.

7. Children’s Privacy

U-Niverse is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected such information, please contact us and we will delete it.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will post the updated policy on this page and update the "Last updated" date. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

9. Contact Us

For privacy-related questions or requests, please contact us via the support URL provided in the app’s listing on the App Store or on our website.
